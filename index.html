Crystals Collector!!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, and lose if your score ends up higher than the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
